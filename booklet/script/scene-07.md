## Section 1 (pages 27-28)

好呀，點解周圍都亂。（舉起手）吖，叮叮魔法又失靈。  
hou2 aa3, dim2 gaai2 zau1 wai4 dou1 lyun6. (geoi2 hei2 sau2) aa1, ding1 ding1 mo4 faat3 jau6 sat1 ling4.

Anyway, the Child Family still didn’t win.

（聲音顫抖）我已經試咗好多次，點解無叮叮魔法，我乜都做唔到，我真係做唔到。  
(sing1 jam1 zin3 dau2) ngo5 ji5 ging1 si3 zo2 hou2 do1 ci3, dim2 gaai2 mou4 ding1 ding1 mo4 faat3, ngo5 mat1 dou1 zou6 m4 dou2, ngo5 zan1 hai6 zou6 m4 dou2.  
(Voice trembling) I’ve tried so many times—without Ding Ding magic, I can’t do anything. I really can’t.


那奇妙雙手，我一向都擁有。  
naa5 kei4 miu6 soeng1 sau2, ngo5 jat1 hoeng3 dou1 jung2 jau5.  
Those wondrous hands—I always had them.


叮一聲得心應手，那微妙手。  
ding1 jat1 seng1 dak1 sam1 jing3 sau2, naa5 mei4 miu6 sau2.  
One ding and everything worked perfectly—those subtle, magical hands.


這就是我擁有，叮一聲狠心退走。  
ze5 zau6 si6 ngo5 jung2 jau5, ding1 jat1 seng1 han2 sam1 teoi3 zau2.  
That was what I had—and with one ding, it turned away without mercy.


曾經的閃亮舞台，如此田地，為何如今。  
cang4 ging1 dik1 sim2 loeng6 mou5 toi4, jyu4 ci2 tin4 dei6, wai6 ho4 jyu4 gam1.  
Once a shining stage—why has it become this now?


我不要，叮一聲，天空塌下來，多孤單。  
ngo5 m4 jiu3, ding1 jat1 seng1, tin1 hung1 taap3 haa6 loi4, do1 gu1 daan1.  
I don’t want this—one ding, and the sky collapses, leaving such loneliness.


只一瞬間，叮一聲，不想再向前。  
zi2 jat1 seon3 gaan1, ding1 jat1 seng1, bat1 soeng2 zoi3 hoeng3 cin4.  
In just a moment—one ding—and I don’t want to move forward anymore.


我真的試過了。  
ngo5 zan1 dik1 si3 gwo3 liu5.  
I really did try.


遙遙，你唔好唔開心。  
jiu4 jiu4, nei5 m4 hou2 m4 hoi1 sam1.  
Yiu-Yiu, don’t be so unhappy.


Baby，relax。  
baby, relax.  
Baby, relax.

nei5 dei6 bei2 ngo5 zing6 haa5 aa1...

周圍，我都唔想喺度吖。  
zau1 wai4, ngo5 dou1 m4 soeng2 hai2 dou6 aa1.  
With this mess all around, I don’t want to stay here either.


小姐喊呀，我想去抹眼呀。  
siu2 ze2 haam3 aa3, ngo5 soeng2 heoi3 mut3 ngaan5 aa3.  
Miss is crying—I want to go wipe her tears.


唔好去住。  
m4 hou2 heoi3 zyu6.  
Don’t go yet.


占士、艾莉，我諗，遙遙小姐會慢慢嘗試無管家的生活。  
zim1 si6, ngaai6 lei6, ngo5 nam2, jiu4 jiu4 siu2 ze2 wui5 maan6 maan6 soeng4 si3 mou4 gun2 gaa1 dik1 sang1 wut6.  
James, Ellie, I think Miss Yiu-Yiu will slowly learn to live without butlers.


失敗都係學習的一部分，除咗小姐有學，你哋個個都有學。  
sat1 baai6 dou1 hai6 hok6 zaap6 dik1 jat1 bou6 fan6, ceoi4 zo2 siu2 ze2 jau5 hok6, nei5 dei6 go3 go3 dou1 jau5 hok6.  
Failure is also part of learning. Not only Miss Yiu-Yiu learns—you all learn too.


真正嘅管家，唔係要主人做晒所有事，而係要慢慢俾佢學識獨立。  
zan1 zing3 ge3 gun2 gaa1, m4 hai6 jiu3 zyu2 jan4 zou6 saai3 so2 jau5 si6, ji4 hai6 jiu3 maan6 maan6 bei2 keoi5 hok6 sik1 duk6 lap6.  
A true butler doesn’t do everything for the master, but helps them gradually learn independence.


（以手指按嘴）嘘！靜靜等。  
(ji5 sau2 zi2 on3 zeoi2) heoi1! zing6 zing6 dang2.  
(Finger to lips) Shh. Wait quietly.


望星空，晚風中；一張發愁眉目有些苦。  
mong6 sing1 hung1, maan5 fung1 zung1; jat1 zoeng1 faat3 sau4 mei4 muk6 jau5 se1 fu2.  
Looking at the starry sky in the evening breeze, a worried face carries quiet pain.


如何是好，想一切為你分擔；你不用驚慌失措。  
jyu4 ho4 si6 hou2, soeng2 jat1 cai3 wai6 nei5 fan1 daam1; nei5 bat1 jung6 ging1 fong1 sat1 cou3.  
What should we do? I want to share everything for you; you don’t need to panic.


有我守護你的喜好，到這刻總會捨不得，願為你去鋪面前路。  
jau5 ngo5 sau2 wu6 nei5 dik1 hei2 hou3, dou3 ze5 hak1 zung2 wui5 se2 bat1 dak1, jyun6 wai6 nei5 heoi3 pou1 min6 cin4 lou6.  
I’m here to guard all that you cherish; even now it’s hard to let go, yet I’ll pave the road ahead for you.


未來日子每段路，仍然存在千萬個變數；相信狠心去放手，讓你步更高。  
mei6 loi4 jat6 zi2 mui5 dyun6 lou6, jing4 jin4 cyun4 zoi6 cin1 maan6 go3 bin3 sou3; soeng1 seon3 han2 sam1 heoi3 fong3 sau2, joeng6 nei5 bou6 gang3 gou1.  
Every stretch of days ahead still holds countless unknowns; trust in the hard choice to let go, so you can rise higher.


靜靜守候，讓我放手，不打擾，就在身邊；不遠走，得失挫折我會守候。  
zing6 zing6 sau2 hau6, joeng6 ngo5 fong3 sau2, bat1 daa2 jiu2, zau6 zoi6 san1 bin1; bat1 jyun5 zau2, dak1 sat1 co3 zit3 ngo5 wui5 sau2 hau6.  
Quietly I’ll keep watch—let me let go, without interrupting, while staying by your side. I won’t go far; through gains, losses, and setbacks, I’ll remain.


盼你磨鍊，我會默默陪伴著你，多少春與秋；這份愛只因有你。  
paan3 nei5 mo4 lin6, ngo5 wui5 mak6 mak6 pui4 bun6 zoek6 nei5, do1 siu2 ceon1 jyu5 cau1; ze5 fan6 oi3 zi2 jan1 jau5 nei5.  
I hope you grow through trial; I’ll quietly stay with you through many springs and autumns—this love exists because of you.


## Section 2 (pages 29-30)

有時，最好的幫忙係退後一步，等佢自己行。  
jau5 si4, zeoi3 hou2 dik1 bong1 mong4 hai6 teoi3 hau6 jat1 bou6, dang2 keoi5 zi6 gei2 haang4.  
Sometimes, the best help is stepping back one step and letting her walk on her own.


你……你喺呢度做吖？  
nei5... nei5 hai2 ni1 dou6 zou6 aa1?  
You... what are you doing here?


（淡淡的）地下好亂。  
(daam6 daam6 dik1) dei6 haa5 hou2 lyun6.  
(Calmly) The floor is a mess.


（接過，低聲）唔該……但係呢啲，應該有人幫我做先啫。  
(zip3 gwo3, dai1 seng1) m4 goi1... daan6 hai6 ni1 di1, jing1 goi1 jau5 jan4 bong1 ngo5 zou6 sin1 ze1.  
(Taking it, softly) Thanks... but these things—someone should be helping me do them first.


（繼續收拾）我每日都要自己執，無人幫我㗎。  
(gai3 zuk6 sau1 sap6) ngo5 mui5 jat6 dou1 jiu3 zi6 gei2 zap1, mou4 jan4 bong1 ngo5 gaa3.  
(Keeps tidying) I have to handle this myself every day. No one helps me.


慢慢就識㗎啦。  
maan6 maan6 zau6 sik1 gaa3 laa1.  
You’ll learn it little by little.


你……乜都識自己做㗎？執袋、抹地、搬……  
nei5... mat1 dou1 sik1 zi6 gei2 zou6 gaa3? zap1 doi2, mut3 dei6, bun1...  
You... can do everything yourself? Pack bags, mop the floor, move things...


但係……我連書包都未識執好。  
daan6 hai6... ngo5 lin4 syu1 baau1 dou1 mei6 sik1 zap1 hou2.  
But... I still don’t even know how to organize my schoolbag properly.


唔識，咪學住做一次；唔識，就做第二次、第三次。你叻過我，一定識。  
m4 sik1, mai6 hok6 zyu6 zou6 jat1 ci3; m4 sik1, zau6 zou6 dai6 ji6 ci3, dai6 saam1 ci3. nei5 lek1 gwo3 ngo5, jat1 ding6 sik1.  
If you don’t know, then learn by doing it once; if not, do it a second and third time. You’re smarter than me—you’ll definitely learn.


其實，我都好羨慕你哋識彈琴、識英文。我淨係識執。  
kei4 sat6, ngo5 dou1 hou2 sin6 mou6 nei5 dei6 sik1 taan4 kam4, sik1 jing1 man4. ngo5 zing6 hai6 sik1 zap1.  
Honestly, I envy that you all can play piano and speak English. I only know how to tidy up.


等陣！  
dang2 zan6!  
Wait!


你可唔可以教我一次呀？教我吖。  
nei5 ho2 m4 ho2 ji5 gaau3 ngo5 jat1 ci3 aa3? gaau3 ngo5 aa1.  
Can you teach me once? Teach me, please.


小志，教埋我吖。  
siu2 zi3, gaau3 maai4 ngo5 aa1.  
Siu-Chi, teach me too.


Baby no... gentleman，我都想學吖。  
baby no... gentleman, ngo5 dou1 soeng2 hok6 aa1.  
Baby no... gentleman—I want to learn too.


（靦腆一笑）唔係教，只係……  
(min5 tin2 jat1 siu3) m4 hai6 gaau3, zi2 hai6...  
(Gives a shy smile) It’s not really teaching, it’s just...


仲有我都唔想用叮叮魔法，我想學……  
zung6 jau5 ngo5 dou1 m4 soeng2 jung6 ding1 ding1 mo4 faat3, ngo5 soeng2 hok6...  
And I don’t want to rely on Ding Ding magic either—I want to learn...


我意思係，唔係教，大家一齊執啦。  
ngo5 ji3 si6 hai6, m4 hai6 gaau3, daai6 gaa1 jat1 cai4 zap1 laa1.  
What I mean is, it’s not teaching—let’s all tidy up together.


你要自我解救。  
nei5 jiu3 zi6 ngo5 gaai2 gau3.  
You have to save yourself.


靠自己雙手。  
kaau3 zi6 gei2 soeng1 sau2.  
Rely on your own two hands.


推開心魔不顫抖。  
teoi1 hoi1 sam1 mo1 bat1 zin3 dau2.  
Push away inner demons and don’t tremble.


要像這小燕，靠自己雙手。  
jiu3 zoeng6 ze5 siu2 jin3, kaau3 zi6 gei2 soeng1 sau2.  
Be like this little swallow—rely on your own two hands.


起居不怕，靈魂迷失。  
hei2 geoi1 bat1 paa3, ling4 wan4 mai4 sat1.  
Don’t fear daily living or losing your way.


無知的失敗少年，不強大。  
mou4 zi1 dik1 sat1 baai6 siu3 nin4, bat1 koeng4 daai6.  
An ignorant, defeated youth is not strong.


徒勞無功，無止境膨大妄為。  
tou4 lou4 mou4 gung1, mou4 zi2 ging2 paang4 daai6 mong5 wai4.  
Futile and fruitless, endlessly swelling into recklessness.


我不想再退後（你要）。  
ngo5 bat1 soeng2 zoi3 teoi3 hau6 (nei5 jiu3).  
I don’t want to step back again (you must).


看我沒用的那雙手（靠你一雙手）。  
hon3 ngo5 mut6 jung6 dik1 naa5 soeng1 sau2 (kaau3 nei5 jat1 soeng1 sau2).  
Look at these useless hands of mine (rely on your own hands).


我不敢去試（也靠你身邊的好友）。  
ngo5 bat1 gam2 heoi3 si3 (jaa5 kaau3 nei5 san1 bin1 dik1 hou2 jau5).  
I don’t dare to try (and rely on good friends beside you).


再嘗試怕出醜（不必怕會出醜）。  
zoi3 soeng4 si3 paa3 ceot1 cau2 (bat1 bit1 paa3 wui5 ceot1 cau2).  
Trying again means fearing embarrassment (you don’t need to fear embarrassment).


我不想再被看透（說要）。  
ngo5 bat1 soeng2 zoi3 bei6 hon3 tau3 (syut3 jiu3).  
I don’t want to be seen through again (say it—).


失去了身邊的手（信自己）。  
sat1 heoi3 liu5 san1 bin1 dik1 sau2 (seon3 zi6 gei2).  
I’ve lost the supporting hands beside me (believe in yourself).


我經已去嘗試，再嘗試。  
ngo5 ging1 ji5 heoi3 soeng4 si3, zoi3 soeng4 si3.  
I already tried—then tried again.


要出走（爭一口氣），你不必走。  
jiu3 ceot1 zau2 (zaang1 jat1 hau2 hei3), nei5 bat1 bit1 zau2.  
Need to break out (fight for one breath of pride), you don’t have to leave.


我不要，叮一聲，天空塌下來。  
ngo5 bat1 jiu3, ding1 jat1 seng1, tin1 hung1 taap3 haa6 loi4.  
I don’t want this—one ding, and the sky comes crashing down.


多孤單（放下你所愛的魔法）。  
do1 gu1 daan1 (fong3 haa5 nei5 so2 oi3 dik1 mo4 faat3).  
Such loneliness (set down the magic you cling to).


只一瞬間（虛如幻），叮一聲不想再向前（錯了也不怕）。  
zi2 jat1 seon3 gaan1 (heoi1 jyu4 waan6), ding1 jat1 seng1 bat1 soeng2 zoi3 hoeng3 cin4 (co3 liu5 jaa5 bat1 paa3).  
In just a moment (all illusion), one ding and I don’t want to move forward (even if wrong, don’t be afraid).


明日誰可以來助我覺醒（求突破，就靠這對手），來開新節奏。  
ming4 jat6 seoi4 ho2 ji5 loi4 zo6 ngo5 gok3 sing2 (kau4 tyut3 po3, zau6 kaau3 ze5 deoi3 sau2), loi4 hoi1 san1 zit3 zau3.  
Who can help awaken me tomorrow (seek a breakthrough, rely on these two hands), and open a new rhythm.


（輕聲）叮叮今次，我唔靠你啦。  
(hing1 seng1) ding1 ding1 gam1 ci3, ngo5 m4 kaau3 nei5 laa1.  
(Softly) Ding Ding, this time I won’t rely on you.
