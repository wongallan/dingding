## Section 1 (pages 11-12)

管家守則第二條專業守則第一節。  
gun2 gaa1 sau2 zak1 dai6 ji6 tiu4 zyun1 jip6 sau2 zak1 dai6 jat1 zit3.  
Butler Code, Article 2, Professional Standards, Section 1.


要有絕對專業的知識，不論烹調食物、清潔打掃、採購家品的認知，同時亦要時刻掌握最新資訊。  
jiu3 jau5 zyut6 deoi3 zyun1 jip6 dik1 zi1 sik1, bat1 leon6 paang1 tiu4 sik6 mat6, cing1 git3 daa2 sou3, coi2 kau3 gaa1 ban2 dik1 jing6 zi1, tung4 si4 jik6 jiu3 si4 hak1 zoeng2 ak1 zeoi3 san1 zi1 seon3.  
You must have fully professional knowledge—from cooking and cleaning to household procurement awareness—and keep up with the latest information at all times.


管家守則第六條工作紀律第四節。  
gun2 gaa1 sau2 zak1 dai6 luk6 tiu4 gung1 zok3 gei2 leot6 dai6 sei3 zit3.  
Butler Code, Article 6, Work Discipline, Section 4.


遵守工作時間、服裝規定、公司規則等。  
zeon1 sau2 gung1 zok3 si4 gaan3, fuk6 zong1 kwai1 ding6, gung1 si1 kwai1 zak1 dang2.  
Observe working hours, dress regulations, company rules, and related policies.


管家守則第十七條安全意識第八節B。  
gun2 gaa1 sau2 zak1 dai6 sap6 cat1 tiu4 on1 cyun4 ji3 sik1 dai6 baat3 zit3 B.  
Butler Code, Article 17, Safety Awareness, Section 8B.


要有一定安全基本知識，了解家居安全、火警預防，同埋災難緊急應變。  
jiu3 jau5 jat1 ding6 on1 cyun4 gei1 bun2 zi1 sik1, liu5 gaai2 gaa1 geoi1 on1 cyun4, fo2 ging2 jyu6 fong4, tung4 maai4 zoi1 naan6 gan2 gap1 jing3 bin3.  
You need fundamental safety knowledge, including home safety, fire prevention, and emergency disaster response.


管家守則第三十八條病患須知第二節A。  
gun2 gaa1 sau2 zak1 dai6 saam1 sap6 baat3 tiu4 beng6 waan6 seoi1 zi1 dai6 ji6 zit3 A.  
Butler Code, Article 38, Illness Guidelines, Section 2A.


當管家患上傳染病，例如傷風、感冒、腸胃炎、水痘或者手足口病嘅時候，要即時休息，隔離自己，絕對唔可以傳染畀少爺小姐。  
dong1 gun2 gaa1 waan6 soeng5 cyun4 jim5 beng6, lai6 jyu4 soeng1 fung1, gam2 mou6, coeng4 wai6 jim4, seoi2 dau6 waak6 ze2 sau2 zuk1 hau2 beng6 ge3 si4 hau6, jiu3 zik1 si4 jau1 sik1, gaak3 lei4 zi6 gei2, zyut6 deoi3 m4 ho2 ji5 cyun4 jim5 bei2 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2.  
If a butler has a contagious illness—like a cold, flu, gastroenteritis, chickenpox, or hand-foot-mouth disease—they must rest immediately, isolate themselves, and never pass infection to the young masters or misses.


管家守則第四十六條私隱保護。  
gun2 gaa1 sau2 zak1 dai6 sei3 sap6 luk6 tiu4 si1 jan2 bou2 wu6.  
Butler Code, Article 46, Privacy Protection.


嚴格履行保密義務，少爺小姐屋企嘅事，絕對唔可以同任何人透露。  
jim4 gaak3 lei5 hang4 bou2 mat6 ji6 mou6, siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 uk1 kei2 ge3 si6, zyut6 deoi3 m4 ho2 ji5 tung4 jam6 ho4 jan4 tau3 lou6.  
Strictly fulfill confidentiality duties; matters in the young masters’ and misses’ homes must never be disclosed to anyone.


各位管家果然非常專業，熟讀管家守則。最近我收到有少爺小姐投訴，話管家間唔中做錯、做漏。  
gok3 wai2 gun2 gaa1 gwo2 jin4 fei1 soeng4 zyun1 jip6, suk6 duk6 gun2 gaa1 sau2 zak1. zeoi3 gan6 ngo5 sau1 dou3 jau5 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 tau4 sou3, waa6 gun2 gaa1 gaan1 m4 zung1 zou6 co3, zou6 lau6.  
You butlers are indeed very professional and know the code well. Recently, though, I’ve received complaints from young masters and misses that butlers sometimes make mistakes or miss tasks.


例如呢一位，竟然因為畀小姐遲起身，而令到小姐遲到。  
lai6 jyu4 ni1 jat1 wai2, ging2 jin4 jan1 wai6 bei2 siu2 ze2 ci4 hei2 san1, ji4 ling6 dou3 siu2 ze2 ci4 dou3.  
For example, this one caused her miss to wake up late, and that made her late.


對、對唔住，我……我唔知醒起身……  
deoi3, deoi3 m4 zyu6, ngo5... ngo5 m4 zi1 seng2 hei2 san1...  
S-sorry... I... I didn’t wake up in time...


身為管家，點可以唔知醒呢？我收到比比小姐投訴，話佢起身使用叮叮魔法時，竟然無管家幫佢揭起張被。  
san1 wai4 gun2 gaa1, dim2 ho2 ji5 m4 zi1 seng2 ne1? ngo5 sau1 dou3 bei2 bei2 siu2 ze2 tau4 sou3, waa6 keoi5 hei2 san1 si2 jung6 ding1 ding1 mo4 faat3 si4, ging2 jin4 mou4 gun2 gaa1 bong1 keoi5 kit3 hei2 zoeng1 bei6.  
As a butler, how can you fail to wake up? I received a complaint from Miss Bibi that when she got up and used Ding Ding Magic, no butler even lifted her blanket.


仲要無管家準備洗面同埋漱口嘅水，令到佢唔知點洗面，仲因為遲食早餐，令到佢飲唔到早餐杯朱古力。  
zung6 jiu3 mou4 gun2 gaa1 zeon2 bei6 sai2 min6 tung4 maai4 sou3 hau2 ge3 seoi2, ling6 dou3 keoi5 m4 zi1 dim2 sai2 min6, zung6 jan1 wai6 ci4 sik6 zou2 caan1, ling6 dou3 keoi5 jam2 m4 dou2 zou2 caan1 bui1 zyu1 gu2 lik6.  
There was no butler to prepare water for washing up and rinsing, she didn’t even know how to start getting ready, and because breakfast was delayed, she didn’t get her breakfast chocolate drink.


我唔係唔起身，我係起唔到身呀，對唔住，我知錯啦。前一晚因為小姐做完數科功課，要我幫佢擦返啲鉛筆痕，仲有做完視藝科功課，要我抹返倒滿一地嘅顏料。個晚我發夢都聽到小姐嗌「叮叮叮」，對唔住。  
ngo5 m4 hai6 m4 hei2 san1, ngo5 hai6 hei2 m4 dou2 san1 aa3, deoi3 m4 zyu6, ngo5 zi1 co3 laa1. cin4 jat1 maan5 jan1 wai6 siu2 ze2 zou6 jyun4 sou3 fo1 gung1 fo3, jiu3 ngo5 bong1 keoi5 caat3 faan1 di1 jyun4 bat1 han4, zung6 jau5 zou6 jyun4 si6 ngai6 fo1 gung1 fo3, jiu3 ngo5 maat3 faan1 dou3 mun5 jat1 dei6 ge3 ngaan4 liu6. go3 maan5 ngo5 faat3 mung6 dou1 teng1 dou2 siu2 ze2 aai3 "ding1 ding1 ding1", deoi3 m4 zyu6.  
It’s not that I refused to get up—I just couldn’t get up. I’m sorry, I know I was wrong. The night before, after Miss finished math homework, I had to erase pencil marks for her, and after art homework I had to clean paint spilled all over the floor. That night, even in my dreams, I heard her calling “Ding ding ding.” I’m sorry.


占士，話畀沙沙拉管家聽，管家守則第六條工作紀律第九節B。  
zim1 si6, waa6 bei2 saa1 saa1 laai1 gun2 gaa1 teng1, gun2 gaa1 sau2 zak1 dai6 luk6 tiu4 gung1 zok3 gei2 leot6 dai6 gau2 zit3 B.  
James, tell Butler Sa-Sa-La: Butler Code, Article 6, Work Discipline, Section 9B.


作為管家，永遠要比少爺小姐早起。因為作為日理萬機嘅少爺小姐，絕對唔可以浪費寶貴時間瞓懶覺。  
zok3 wai4 gun2 gaa1, wing5 jyun5 jiu3 bei2 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 zou2 hei2. jan1 wai6 zok3 wai4 jat6 lei5 maan6 gei1 ge3 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2, zyut6 deoi3 m4 ho2 ji5 long6 fai3 bou2 gwai3 si4 gaan3 fan3 laan5 gaau3.  
As a butler, you must always rise earlier than your young master or miss. Their time is precious, so they must never waste it sleeping in.


管家一定要喺指定時間叫醒少爺小姐，為佢哋熨好校服，而且喺少爺小姐擦牙洗面嘅時候，熱騰騰嘅早餐已經準時上枱。  
gun2 gaa1 jat1 ding6 jiu3 hai2 zi2 ding6 si4 gaan3 giu3 seng2 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2, wai4 keoi5 dei6 wan6 hou2 haau6 fuk6, ji5 cing2 hai2 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 caat3 ngaa4 sai2 min6 ge3 si4 hau6, jit6 tang4 tang4 ge3 zou2 caan1 ji5 ging1 zeon2 si4 soeng5 toi2.  
A butler must wake the young masters and misses at the designated time, prepare their school uniforms, and ensure hot breakfast is already served while they brush up.


作為管家，最好幾時起身？  
zok3 wai4 gun2 gaa1, zeoi3 hou2 gei2 si4 hei2 san1?  
As a butler, what’s the best time to wake up?


最好少爺小姐醒之前兩個鐘頭。  
zeoi3 hou2 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 seng2 zi1 cin4 loeng5 go3 zung1 tau4.  
Ideally, two hours before the young master or miss wakes.


唔該晒，占士。仲有麥芬尼，係安娜小姐有少少意見。  
m4 goi1 saai3, zim1 si6. zung6 jau5 mak6 fan1 nei4, hai6 on1 no4 siu2 ze2 jau5 siu2 siu2 ji3 gin3.  
Thank you, James. Also, regarding Mak-Fanny, Miss Anna has a small concern.


係！（驚）我有做錯？  
hai6! (ging1) ngo5 jau5 zou6 co3?  
Yes! (Startled) Did I do something wrong?


唔係投訴，係安娜小姐有少少意見。佢提到你上次幫佢視藝堂準備顏色調盤，每一項分類都太仔細，但唔係好搵到一隻普通綠色。  
m4 hai6 tau4 sou3, hai6 on1 no4 siu2 ze2 jau5 siu2 siu2 ji3 gin3. keoi5 tai4 dou3 nei5 soeng6 ci3 bong1 keoi5 si6 ngai6 tong4 zeon2 bei6 ngaan4 sik1 tiu4 pun4, mui5 jat1 hong6 fan1 leoi6 dou1 taai3 zai2 sai3, daan6 m4 hai6 hou2 wan2 dou2 jat1 zek3 pou2 tung1 luk6 sik1.  
It’s not a complaint—Miss Anna just has a suggestion. Last time when you prepared her art-class color trays, every category was too detailed, so it became hard to find a normal green.


吓！  
haa2!  
Huh?!


我知道啦，一定係你做多咗啦。  
ngo5 zi1 dou6 laa1, jat1 ding6 hai6 nei5 zou6 do1 zo2 laa1.  
I get it—you definitely overdid it.


即係點呀？  
zik1 hai6 dim2 aa3?  
What does that mean exactly?


即係你幫小姐分類簡單返少少就得喇。  
zik1 hai6 nei5 bong1 siu2 ze2 fan1 leoi6 gaan2 daan1 faan1 siu2 siu2 zau6 dak1 laa3.  
It means you should keep the classification for Miss a little simpler.


達爾少爺成日都同我講：Baby，relax。  
daat6 ji5 siu3 je5 sing4 jat6 dou1 tung4 ngo5 gong2: Baby, relax.  
Young Master Dar always tells me: “Baby, relax.”


作為一位管家，幫少爺小姐做工作，一定要切合返年紀，唔好複雜自己，簡單啲。  
zok3 wai4 jat1 wai2 gun2 gaa1, bong1 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 zou6 gung1 zok3, jat1 ding6 jiu3 cit3 hap6 faan1 nin4 gei3, m4 hou2 fuk1 zaap6 zi6 gei2, gaan2 daan1 di1.  
As a butler helping young masters and misses, your work should fit their age—don’t overcomplicate it; keep it simple.


完全正確。要時刻記住，少爺小姐每次「叮」一聲嘅時候，作為管家都要即刻幫手。好喇，今個月管家會議重點，需要跟進。  
jyun4 cyun4 zing3 kok3. jiu3 si4 hak1 gei3 zyu6, siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 mui5 ci3 "ding1" jat1 seng1 ge3 si4 hau6, zok3 wai4 gun2 gaa1 dou1 jiu3 zik1 hak1 bong1 sau2. hou2 laa3, gam1 go3 jyut6 gun2 gaa1 wui6 ji5 zung6 dim2, seoi1 jiu3 gan1 zeon3.  
Exactly right. Always remember: whenever a young master or miss says “ding,” a butler must help immediately. Alright—this month’s meeting priorities now need follow-up.


唔好意思，因為我新做管家，我想問下，整蛋嗰陣，糖要以咩角度去落，先至最適合少爺小姐？  
m4 hou2 ji3 si3, jan1 wai6 ngo5 san1 zou6 gun2 gaa1, ngo5 seung2 man6 haa5, zing2 daan2 go2 zan6, tong4 jiu3 ji5 me1 gok3 dou6 heoi3 lok6, sin1 zi3 zeoi3 sik1 hap6 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2?  
Sorry—I’m new as a butler, so I want to ask: when making eggs, at what angle should sugar be added so it best suits the young masters and misses?


占士，示一次。  
zim1 si6, si6 jat1 ci3.  
James, demonstrate once.


好。記住，一定要打完蛋之後起手，砂糖以90度角埋去，就最perfect啦。  
hou2. gei3 zyu6, jat1 ding6 jiu3 daa2 jyun4 daan2 zi1 hau6 hei2 sau2, saa1 tong4 ji5 gau2 sap6 dou6 gok3 maai4 heoi3, zau6 zeoi3 perfect laa1.  
Okay. Remember: after beating the eggs, start by adding sugar at a 90-degree angle—that’s the most perfect way.


唔該晒，下題。  
m4 goi1 saai3, haa6 tai4.  
Thanks, next question.


我想問，冬天就完，要收起冬天嘅被，應該點摺？  
ngo5 seung2 man6, dung1 tin1 zau6 jyun4, jiu3 sau1 hei2 dung1 tin1 ge3 bei6, jing1 goi1 dim2 zip3?  
I want to ask: when winter ends and we store winter quilts, how should they be folded?


麥芬尼，示一次。  
mak6 fan1 nei4, si6 jat1 ci3.  
Mak-Fanny, demonstrate once.


唔該晒各位管家，進入下個議題之前，畀大家休息一陣。  
m4 goi1 saai3 gok3 wai2 gun2 gaa1, zeon3 jap6 haa6 go3 ji5 tai4 zi1 cin4, bei2 daai6 gaa1 jau1 sik1 jat1 zan6.  
Thank you, butlers. Before we move to the next topic, everyone take a short break.


今日個冷氣好似有啲……  
gam1 jat6 go3 laang5 hei3 hou2 ci5 jau5 di1...  
The air conditioning today feels a bit...


我都覺得呀。  
ngo5 dou1 gok3 dak1 aa3.  
I feel that too.


聽講最近有一種嚴重嘅病毒，中咗之後會成日打乞……  
teng1 gong2 zeoi3 gan6 jau5 jat1 zung2 jim4 zung6 ge3 beng6 duk6, zung3 zo2 zi1 hau6 wui5 sing4 jat6 daa2 hat1...  
I heard there’s a serious virus going around lately—after you catch it, you keep sneezing...


## Section 2 (pages 13-14)

聽講最近有一種嚴重嘅病毒，中咗之後會成日打乞嗤……（再大聲）乞嗤……  
teng1 gong2 zeoi3 gan6 jau5 jat1 zung2 jim4 zung6 ge3 beng6 duk6, zung3 zo2 zi1 hau6 wui5 sing4 jat6 daa2 hat1 ci1... (zoi3 daai6 seng1) hat1 ci1...  
I heard there’s a serious virus going around lately—once you catch it, you keep sneezing... (louder) A-choo...


如果唔小心感染病毒，我哋咪要隔離，照顧唔到少爺小姐？  
jyu4 gwo2 m4 siu2 sam1 gam2 jim5 beng6 duk6, ngo5 dei6 mai6 jiu3 gaak3 lei4, ziu3 gu3 m4 dou2 siu3 je5 siu2 ze2?  
If we accidentally get infected, does that mean we have to quarantine and can’t look after the young masters and misses?


你打乞嗤？  
nei5 daa2 hat1 ci1?  
Did you just sneeze?


各位管家，因為懷疑你哋中咗管家界絕症——懶惰感冒，管家會議而家進入緊急狀態。  
gok3 wai2 gun2 gaa1, jan1 wai6 waai4 ji4 nei5 dei6 zung3 zo2 gun2 gaa1 gaai3 zyut6 zing3 -- laan5 do6 gam2 mou6, gun2 gaa1 wui6 ji5 ji4 gaa1 zeon3 jap6 gan2 gap1 zong6 taai3.  
Attention, butlers: because you are suspected of contracting the butler world’s terminal illness—Lazy Flu—the butler meeting is now entering emergency mode.


個個都唔可以離開呢間房。  
go3 go3 dou1 m4 ho2 ji5 lei4 hoi1 ni1 gaan1 fong4.  
No one is allowed to leave this room.


吓！少爺小姐點算呀？  
haa2! siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 dim2 syun3 aa3?  
What?! Then what about the young masters and misses?


手腳也軟。  
sau2 goek3 jaa5 jyun5.  
My hands and feet are going weak.


人像已經失控。  
jan4 zoeng6 ji5 ging1 sat1 hung3.  
It feels like everything is already out of control.


我頭極痛。  
ngo5 tau4 gik6 tung3.  
My head hurts terribly.


如像墮進惡夢。  
jyu4 zoeng6 do6 zeon3 ok3 mung6.  
It’s like falling into a nightmare.


怎會接觸這種奇異病菌。  
zam2 wui5 zip3 zuk1 ze5 zung2 kei4 ji6 beng6 gwan2.  
How did we come into contact with such a strange germ?


心煩無奈，累了未放鬆。  
sam1 faan4 mou4 noi6, leoi6 liu5 mei6 fong3 sung1.  
Restless and helpless—tired, but still not at ease.


隔離被困，小姐晚餐如何能送。  
gaak3 lei4 bei6 kwan3, siu2 ze2 maan5 caan1 jyu4 ho4 nang4 sung3.  
Trapped in quarantine—how can we deliver Miss’s dinner?


命運被愚弄。  
ming6 wan6 bei6 jyu4 lung6.  
Fate is making fools of us.


小姐慣常被我寵。  
siu2 ze2 gwaan3 soeng4 bei6 ngo5 cung2.  
Miss is usually cared for by me.


曬被鋪床，涼又凍。  
saai3 bei6 pou1 cong4, loeng4 jau6 dung3.  
Airing quilts and making beds—cold and chilly.


穿上校衫，刷牙梳洗。  
cyun1 soeng5 haau6 saam1, caat3 ngaa4 so1 sai2.  
Put on school uniform, brush teeth, wash up.


沒我在，放學發現漏，擔心她哭叫難自控。  
mut6 ngo5 zoi6, fong3 hok6 faat3 jin6 lau6, daam1 sam1 taa1 huk1 giu3 naan4 zi6 hung3.  
Without me there, after school she may find things missing—I worry she’ll cry out and won’t be able to calm down.


早日結束，能為我解封。  
zou2 jat6 git3 cuk1, nang4 wai4 ngo5 gaai2 fung1.  
Let this end soon, so I can be released.


擺脫這惡夢。  
baai2 tyut3 ze5 ok3 mung6.  
Break free from this nightmare.
