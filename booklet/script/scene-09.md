## Section 1 (pages 35-36)

各位觀眾，「愛叮堡榮譽」頒獎典禮即將開始～
gok3 wai6 gun1 zung3, "oi3 ding1 bou2 wing4 jyu6" pun1 zoeng2 din2 lai5 zik1 zoeng1 hoi1 ci2~
Ladies and gentlemen, the "Love-Ding Honor" awards ceremony is about to begin~


（看向小台）咦？榮譽呢？（周圍搵）咦，唔見咗？
(hon3 hoeng3 siu2 toi4) ji1? wing4 jyu6 ne1? (zau1 wai4 wan2) ji1, m4 gin3 zo2?
(Looks toward the small stage) Huh? Where’s the Honor? (Looks around) Hey—it’s gone?


（不好意思）其實……我唔記得擺返榮譽。
(bat1 hou2 ji3 si1) kei4 sat6... ngo5 m4 gei3 dak1 baai2 faan1 wing4 jyu6.
(Embarrassed) Actually... I forgot to put the Honor back.


（拿出平板電腦）我知道，根據電腦地圖顯示，遺失榮譽方位係……Oh…No signal…
(naa4 ceot1 ping4 baan2 din6 nou5) ngo5 zi1 dou6, gan1 geoi3 din6 nou5 dei6 tou4 hin2 si6, wai4 sat1 wing4 jyu6 fong1 wai6 hai6... Oh... No signal...
(Pulls out a tablet) I know—according to the map display, the lost Honor is located at... Oh... no signal...


（細聲）少爺少爺，快啲叮叮。
(sai3 seng1) siu3 je5 siu3 je5, faai3 di1 ding1 ding1.
(Whispers) Young master, young master—quick, use a ding.


我嚟，我嚟。
ngo5 lai4, ngo5 lai4.
I’ll do it, I’ll do it.


（即時制止）Stop！大家禁叮三分鐘，用雙眼同腦先。
(zik1 si4 zai3 zi2) Stop! daai6 gaa1 gam3 ding1 saam1 fan1 zung1, jung6 soeng1 ngaan5 tung4 nou5 sin1.
(Stops them at once) Stop! No dinging for three minutes—use your eyes and your brains first.


各位同學，三分鐘內唔准用叮叮魔法。
gok3 wai6 tung4 hok6, saam1 fan1 zung1 noi6 m4 zeon2 jung6 ding1 ding1 mo4 faat3.
Students, no Ding Ding Magic for the next three minutes.


任務：自己搵，自己解決。
jam6 mou6: zi6 gei2 wan2, zi6 gei2 gaai2 kyut3.
Mission: find it yourselves, solve it yourselves.


管家只可以提醒同埋引導，不可出手。
gun2 gaa1 zi2 ho2 ji5 tai4 sing2 tung4 maai4 jan5 dou6, bat1 ho2 ceot1 sau2.
Butlers may only remind and guide—you may not step in.


點算呀？
dim2 syun3 aa3?
What do we do?


有方法！「三步找法」！
jau5 fong1 faat3! "saam1 bou6 zaau2 faat3"!
There’s a way! The "three-step search"!


第一，最後出現喺邊？
dai6 jat1, zeoi3 hau6 ceot1 jin6 hai2 bin1?
First: where did it appear last?


第二，最易忽略位置。
dai6 ji6, zeoi3 ji6 fat1 loek6 wai6 zi6.
Second: check the easiest places to overlook.


第三，搵最高同埋最低位。
dai6 saam1, wan2 zeoi3 gou1 tung4 maai4 zeoi3 dai1 wai6.
Third: search the highest and the lowest spots.


（指揮）安娜，你查最窄嘅位；達爾，你負責高位；我同比比負責角落同埋低位。
(zi2 fai1) on1 no4, nei5 caa4 zeoi3 zaak3 ge3 wai6; daat6 ji5, nei5 fu6 zaak3 gou1 wai6; ngo5 tung4 bei2 bei2 fu6 zaak3 gok3 lok6 tung4 maai4 dai1 wai6.
(Directing) Anna, you check the tightest spots; Dar, you take the high spots; Bibi and I take the corners and low spots.


（平靜）記住要放回原位，噉先唔會越搵越亂。
(ping4 zing6) gei3 zyu6 jiu3 fong3 wui4 jyun4 wai6, gam2 sin1 m4 wui5 jyut6 wan2 jyut6 lyun6.
(Calmly) Remember to put things back where they belong, or the more you search, the messier it gets.


（蹲低）咦，呢個係失物認領箱。（打開）嘩！好多糖紙同朱古力紙！
(deon1 dai1) ji1, ni1 go3 hai6 sat1 mat6 jing6 ling5 soeng1. (daa2 hoi1) waa1! hou2 do1 tong4 zi2 tung4 zyu1 gu2 lik6 zi2!
(Crouches down) Huh, this is a lost-and-found box. (Opens it) Wow! So many candy wrappers and chocolate wrappers!


（眼尖）等等，呢個袋上面寫住「榮譽」？
(ngaan5 zim1) dang2 dang2, ni1 go3 doi2 soeng6 min6 se2 zyu6 "wing4 jyu6"?
(Sharp-eyed) Wait—this bag has "Honor" written on it?


（掀布袋）搵到啦！榮譽喺度！
(hin1 bou3 doi2) wan2 dou3 laa1! wing4 jyu6 hai2 dou6!
(Lifts the cloth bag) Found it! The Honor is here!


個榮譽點解會喺失物箱？
go3 wing4 jyu6 dim2 gaai2 wui5 hai2 sat1 mat6 soeng1?
How did the Honor end up in the lost-and-found box?


（向比比，小聲）你尋日練歌嗰陣，借咗做道具。
(hoeng3 bei2 bei2, siu2 seng1) nei5 cam4 jat6 lin6 go1 go2 zan6, ze3 zo2 zou6 dou6 geoi6.
(To Bibi, softly) Yesterday during rehearsal, you borrowed it as a prop.


（尷尬一笑）嗯啊……藝術需要神秘感嘛。
(gaan3 gaai3 jat1 siu3) m4 aa3... ngai6 seot6 seoi1 jiu3 san4 bei6 gam2 maa3.
(Awkward smile) Mm... art needs a sense of mystery, right?


個榮譽污糟，邊個負責擦返乾淨？
go3 wing4 jyu6 wu1 zou1, bin1 go3 fu6 zaak3 caat3 faan1 gon1 zing6?
The Honor is dirty—who will clean it up?


（舉手）交畀我負責拋光同埋上蠟，唔使三十秒，即刻閃到你都認唔到。
(geoi2 sau2) gaau1 bei2 ngo5 fu6 zaak3 paau1 gwong1 tung4 maai4 soeng6 laat6, m4 sai2 saam1 sap6 miu5, zik1 hak1 sim2 dou3 nei5 dou1 jing6 m4 dou2.
(Raises hand) Leave polishing and waxing to me—under thirty seconds, and it’ll shine so much you won’t even recognize it.


（舉手，微笑）等我嚟。
(geoi2 sau2, mei4 siu3) dang2 ngo5 lai4.
(Raises hand, smiling) Let me do it.


我都！
ngo5 dou1!
Me too!


（遞抹布）一人一塊抹布，由上到下、順一個方向，就唔會花。
(dai6 mut3 bou3) jat1 jan4 jat1 faai3 mut3 bou3, jau4 soeng6 dou3 haa6, seon6 jat1 go3 fong1 hoeng3, zau6 m4 wui5 faa1.
(Hands out cloths) One cloth per person; wipe top to bottom in one direction, and it won’t streak.


（指令）分兩組，三個擦左，三個擦右。
(zi2 ling6) fan1 loeng5 zou2, saam1 go3 caat3 zo2, saam1 go3 caat3 jau6.
(Giving orders) Split into two teams—three wipe left, three wipe right.


知道。
zi1 dou6.
Got it.


（看向管家）見到未？最強魔法唔係「叮」，而係郁手去做。
(hon3 hoeng3 gun2 gaa1) gin3 dou2 mei6? zeoi3 koeng4 mo4 faat3 m4 hai6 "ding1", ji4 hai6 juk1 sau2 heoi3 zou6.
(Looks at the butlers) See that? The strongest magic isn’t a "ding"—it’s getting your hands moving and doing it.


而家公布結果。
ji4 gaa1 gung1 bou3 git3 gwo2.
Now we announce the result.


（舉手，小聲）當然係……
(geoi2 sau2, siu2 seng1) dong1 jin4 hai6...
(Raises hand, whispers) Of course it is...


（低聲）低調、低調！
(dai1 seng1) dai1 diu6, dai1 diu6!
(Whispering) Low-key, low-key!


今年「愛叮堡榮譽」決賽都係無人贏。
gam1 nin4 "oi3 ding1 bou2 wing4 jyu6" kyut3 coi3 dou1 hai6 mou4 jan4 jeng4.
In this year’s "Love-Ding Honor" final, there is no winner.


## Section 2 (pages 37-38)

吓？即係我唔係第一？
haa2? zik1 hai6 ngo5 m4 hai6 dai6 jat1?
Huh? So I’m not first place?


（忍笑）你先整唔見個榮譽。
(jan2 siu3) nei5 sin1 zing2 m4 gin3 go3 wing4 jyu6.
(Holding back a laugh) You were the one who lost the Honor first.


（吸口氣，由硬撐變放低）好啦，我知道啦……對唔住！我決定我以後唔會叫人做。
(kap1 hau2 hei3, jau4 ngaang6 caang2 bin3 fong3 dai1) hou2 laa1, ngo5 zi1 dou6 laa1... deoi3 m4 zyu6! ngo5 kyut3 ding6 ngo5 ji5 hau6 m4 wui5 giu3 jan4 zou6.
(Takes a breath, softening) Alright, I get it... I’m sorry! I’ve decided I won’t order people around anymore.


（點頭）我都知：我唔可以成日靠「叮」，有啲嘢要識自己做。
(dim2 tau4) ngo5 dou1 zi1: ngo5 m4 ho2 ji5 sing4 jat6 kaau3 "ding1", jau5 di1 je5 jiu3 sik1 zi6 gei2 zou6.
(Nods) I know too: I can’t rely on “ding” all the time—some things I need to do myself.


所謂「人最強的魔法」，唔係擁有一個魔法；而係學識自立、合群、同埋點樣同人相處。
so2 wai6 "jan4 zeoi3 koeng4 dik1 mo4 faat3", m4 hai6 jung2 jau5 jat1 go3 mo4 faat3; ji4 hai6 hok6 sik1 zi6 lap6, hap6 kwan4, tung4 maai4 dim2 joeng6 tung4 jan4 soeng1 cyu5.
The so-called “strongest human magic” is not possessing one spell; it is learning independence, teamwork, and how to get along with others.


所以今年新增兩個大獎。
so2 ji5 gam1 nin4 zan1 zang1 loeng5 go3 daai6 zoeng2.
So this year, we are adding two major awards.


第一個，「成長自立大獎」，由遙遙、安娜、達爾得。
dai6 jat1 go3, "sing4 zoeng2 zi6 lap6 daai6 zoeng2", jau4 jiu4 jiu4, on1 no4, daat6 ji5 dak1.
The first, the “Growth and Independence Award,” goes to Yiu-Yiu, Anna, and Dar.


因為佢哋唔怕失敗，敢作嘗試。
jan1 wai6 keoi5 dei6 m4 paa3 sat1 baai6, gam2 zok3 soeng4 si3.
Because they are not afraid of failure and dare to try.


而第二個獎，就係「同行勇氣大獎」，得獎者係比比。
ji4 dai6 ji6 go3 zoeng2, zau6 hai6 "tung4 hang4 jung5 hei3 daai6 zoeng2", dak1 zoeng2 ze2 hai6 bei2 bei2.
And the second award is the “Courage to Walk Together Award,” and the winner is Bibi.


因為佢肯認錯和改。
jan1 wai6 keoi5 hang2 jing6 co3 wo4 goi2.
Because she is willing to admit mistakes and change.


（戴好後，向眾人）我唔再用「叮叮」去推開人；我想用雙手去拉近人。
(daai3 hou2 hau6, hoeng3 zung3 jan4) ngo5 m4 zoi3 jung6 "ding1 ding1" heoi3 teoi1 hoi1 jan4; ngo5 soeng2 jung6 soeng1 sau2 heoi3 laai1 gan6 jan4.
(After putting it on, to everyone) I won’t use “ding ding” to push people away anymore; I want to use my hands to pull people closer.


（小聲）我可唔可以繼續做好朋友？
(siu2 seng1) ngo5 ho2 m4 ho2 ji5 gai3 zuk6 zou6 hou2 pang4 jau5?
(Softly) Can I keep being your good friend?


仲有我！
zung6 jau5 ngo5!
And me too!


（笑）可以，不過要一齊執返好個失物箱。
(siu3) ho2 ji5, bat1 gwo3 jiu3 jat1 cai4 zap1 faan1 hou2 go3 sat1 mat6 soeng1.
(Smiles) Yes—but we all have to tidy the lost-and-found box together.


（走前一步）今日，你哋學識自己行；我哋學識退後守候。由依賴，走到自立；由命令，走到合作。
(zau2 cin4 jat1 bou6) gam1 jat6, nei5 dei6 hok6 sik1 zi6 gei2 haang4; ngo5 dei6 hok6 sik1 teoi3 hau6 sau2 hau6. jau4 ji1 laai6, zau2 dou3 zi6 lap6; jau4 ming6 ling6, zau2 dou3 hap6 zok3.
(Steps forward) Today, you learned to walk on your own; we learned to step back and stay watchful. From dependence to independence; from commands to cooperation.


一齊，用最強的魔法，由愛，加上勇氣，再加上實踐，向前行。
jat1 cai4, jung6 zeoi3 koeng4 dik1 mo4 faat3, jau4 oi3, gaa1 soeng6 jung5 hei3, zoi3 gaa1 soeng6 sat6 cin2, hoeng3 cin4 haang4.
Together, with the strongest magic—love, plus courage, plus practice—we move forward.
