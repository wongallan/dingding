## Section 1 (pages 31-32)

孩子總會跌低過。

Children will always stumble and fall.

拍拍手，休息唱首歌。

With you by my side.

The morning air is really fresh! The flowers here are all in bloom and smell wonderful!


Baby！（吸一口氣）聞到呢陣花香，真係病痛都少一半。如果每日都可以好似今日咁就好啦。

Huh? You actually counted that?


我今朝擦花樽，擦到啲花都想同我投訴。

我抹望遠鏡，都抹到見到宇宙呀。
ngo5 mut3 mong6 jyun5 geng3, dou1 mut3 dou3 gin3 dou2 jyu5 zau6 aa3.
I cleaned the telescope so hard I can see the universe.


（伸懶腰）好少可以寫意，通常呢個鐘數，我都係跑緊步。
(san1 laan5 jiu1) hou2 siu2 ho2 ji5 se2 ji3, tung1 soeng4 ni1 go3 zung1 sou3, ngo5 dou1 hai6 paau2 gan2 bou6.
(Stretches) It’s rare to relax like this. Usually at this hour, I’m running around.


唔對路，占士！平時呢個時間，我哋應該聽到「叮叮叮」，但今日好靜。
m4 deoi3 lou6, zim1 si6! ping4 si4 ni1 go3 si4 gaan3, ngo5 dei6 jing1 goi1 teng1 dou2 "ding1 ding1 ding1", daan6 gam1 jat6 hou2 zing6.
Something’s off, James! At this time normally we should hear “ding ding ding,” but today is too quiet.


係呀，平時小姐會「叮叮叮」要飲水、要揭書、要抹眼鏡、要玩鬼口水。
hai6 aa3, ping4 si4 siu2 ze2 wui5 "ding1 ding1 ding1" jiu3 jam2 seoi2, jiu3 kit3 syu1, jiu3 mut3 ngaan5 geng2, jiu3 waan2 gwai2 hau2 seoi2.
Right—normally Miss goes “ding ding ding” for water, to turn pages, to wipe her glasses, to play all sorts of silly things.


係呀，仲會唔記得帶書，要我返屋企攞。

Exactly, and she even forgets her books and makes me run home to fetch them.


小姐會唔會有事？
siu2 ze2 wui5 m4 wui5 jau5 si6?
Could Miss be in trouble?


冷靜。少爺小姐暫時唔用叮叮魔法，想試下用自己對手做，噉咪畀佢哋試下。
laang5 zing6. siu3 je5 siu2 ze2 zaam6 si4 m4 jung6 ding1 ding1 mo4 faat3, soeng2 si3 haa5 jung6 zi6 gei2 deoi3 sau2 zou6, gam2 mai6 bei2 keoi5 dei6 si3 haa5.

嘩，好神奇呀！快啲嚟睇下……

Wow, that’s amazing! Come quickly and look...


真係好神奇吖，遙遙小姐今朝自己執書包返學。等我睇下個書包先……中文、英文、數學、常識都冇帶漏到書呀。
zan1 hai6 hou2 san4 kei4 aa1, jiu4 jiu4 siu2 ze2 gam1 ziu1 zi6 gei2 zap1 syu1 baau1 faan1 hok6. dang2 ngo5 tai2 haa5 go3 syu1 baau1 sin1... zung1 man4, jing1 man4, sou3 hok6, soeng4 sik1 dou1 mou5 daai3 lau6 dou3 syu1 aa3.

我見到達爾少爺識起個水樽飲水啦，同埋今朝可以自己一個人用五分鐘扣晒啲鈕，冇扣錯！
ngo5 gin3 dou2 daat6 ji5 siu3 je5 sik1 hei2 go3 seoi2 zeon1 jam2 seoi2 laa1, tung4 maai4 gam1 ziu1 ho2 ji5 zi6 gei2 jat1 go3 jan4 jung6 ng5 fan1 zung1 kau3 saai3 di1 nau2, mou5 kau3 co3!
I saw Young Master Dar open his water bottle and drink by himself, and this morning he even buttoned everything on his own in five minutes—with no mistakes!


比比小姐都係呀，以前每朝早叫佢起身都要二十分鐘；而家叫兩、三聲就起身啦，仲唔會嬲。
bei2 bei2 siu2 ze2 dou1 hai6 aa3, ji5 cin4 mui5 ziu1 zou2 giu3 keoi5 hei2 san1 dou1 jiu3 ji6 sap6 fan1 zung1; ji4 gaa1 giu3 loeng5, saam1 seng1 zau6 hei2 san1 laa1, zung6 m4 wui5 nau1.
Miss Bibi too—before, waking her every morning took twenty minutes; now two or three calls and she gets up, and she doesn’t get angry.


仲有呀，小姐食完飯會自己洗碗啦，而家仲未打爛。

等等，你哋有冇聽到奇怪聲呀？
dang2 dang2, nei5 dei6 jau5 mou5 teng1 dou2 kei4 gwaai3 seng1 aa3?
Wait—did you hear a strange sound?


吖，咩聲呀？
aa3, me1 seng1 aa3?
Ah? What sound?


好似有人大聲叫緊？
hou2 ci5 jau5 jan4 daai6 seng1 giu3 gan2?

（攞回望遠鏡）咦？小姐離開課室去邊呀？
(lo2 wui4 mong6 jyun5 geng3) ji1? siu2 ze2 lei4 hoi1 fo3 sat1 heoi3 bin1 aa3?
(Takes back the telescope) Huh? Where did Miss go after leaving the classroom?


（搶去艾莉望遠鏡）Baby，少爺呢？
(coeng2 heoi3 ngaai6 lei6 mong6 jyun5 geng3) baby, siu3 je5 ne1?
(Snatches Ellie’s telescope) Baby, where’s Young Master?


要唔要跟實佢？
jiu3 m4 jiu3 gan1 sat6 keoi5?
Should we tail them closely?


開直昇機模式，上面盤旋。

停！（一拍）成日盤旋，少爺小姐點學獨立？記唔記得管家守則第七：唔好衝動，冷靜觀察……或者，唔止少爺小姐要學，我哋都要學。

zim1 si6, dak1 laa3, ngo5 dei6 gai3 zuk6 jam2 caa4 laa1.
James, alright already—let’s continue our tea.


我想知你頭先到底聽到係咩聲？
ngo5 soeng2 zi1 nei5 tau4 sin1 dou3 dai2 teng1 dou2 hai6 me1 seng1?
I want to know what sound you actually heard just now.

噓！畀佢哋學習。
